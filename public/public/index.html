Getting Started

  1. Go to Settings and upload your M3U playlist file
  2. Optionally upload an XMLTV EPG file for program schedules
  3. Navigate to Live TV, Movies, or Series to browse content
  4. Click any item to play it in the Netflix-style player
  5. Use the search bar to find specific channels or shows

Tip: Group your channels using group-title="Category Name" in your M3U file for better organization
